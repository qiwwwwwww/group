About Us

Who We Are?

We are a group of master students from Imperial College, currently working on analysing and presenting electricity disaggregation data.

What We Do?

We set up a platform for any users to upload their electricity usage data and compare theirs with our exclusive dataset across the UK with ease

Why We Do It?

We wish to allow more users to understand the proportion of their electricity usage every day and encourage them to take action to reduce their electricity usage.

Functions
